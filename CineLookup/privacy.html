Privacy Policy for CineLookup

CineLookup does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All searches are performed locally in your browser using the selected text. The extension does not track user activity, access browsing history, or communicate with any external servers.

CineLookup does not use cookies, analytics, or third-party scripts. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

If you have any questions, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
